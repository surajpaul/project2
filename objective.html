Our Objectives

Chaudhary Pawan Singh Educational, Welfare, Cultural & Charitable Society

    Our objectives are as follow:

  • 1. To create a sense of belonging, co-operation, mutual harmony love and affection amongst the member and general public.
  • 2. To promote the cause of national Integration and unity of India and to fight against the forces of separatism in India.
  • 3. To render services to the men, women, children & Youths of the country for their actual moral, social and rights.
  • 4. To arrange establish and run primary, middle and/ or higher secondary School and /or Vocational School/Institutions and to provide the general education to the students of all sections and also to bring the education of best standard within the reach of poor and backwards areas, specially in the different rural areas of the Delhi.
  • 5. To promote literacy, cultural and other social Activities by Awareness programmes, Adult education Classes, Lectures, Essay Completions Exhibitions Symposiums, Cultural programs, press conferences and Seminars.
  • 6. To carry on community development programs/activities and also construct and develop the Community Halls, Club Old age Home, Charitable Dispensaries, Hospital, Libraries, reading rooms, Play Grounds, Yoga Training Centers, Drama Stage and other training and research institute for the attainment of Aims and object of the society.
  • 7. To make adequate arrangements and start/run the rehabilitation center for destitute, widows, old men & women, poor, handicapped, and for mentally retarded persons for their social upliftment.
  • 8. To open the Institutes & hostels for the Normal, handicapped, deaf, dumb and blind etc.
  • 9. To take-up effective, reasonable and lawful steps for the solution of the problems relating to blind, handicapped, mentally retarded, deaf, dumb and other disabled, by providing them all required facilities.
  • 10. To start various programs against child labour and take-up effective but reasonable and lawful steps in this connection for the welfare of children.
  • 11. To provide free legal, medical and socio-economic help/assistance to the poor and needy persons.
  • 12. To conduct training and research programs for control of the pollution, polluted diseases and to maintain/develop/source the Herbs. Forests, Environment, Species of Animals, Bird, wild life etc.
  • 13. To help people in the pre-retirement stage to prepare for life after retirement.
  • 14. To create a forum for Senior Citizens where they can exchange their various views and experiences, which may be, make available to younger generation.
  • 15. To provide information and education/useful knowledge and the medical advise, to general public regarding the voices of the society (like smack, Cigarette/Biri smoking, Alcohol AIDS) through holding awareness camps for De-Addiction and also start/organize the lectures by expert team of Doctors, Journalists and other learned persons.
  • 16. To open a center for diagnostic curative and research of Cancer, Aids and other deadly diseases.
  • 17. To establish and maintain institutions for the handicapped, blind, poor, SC & STs, Backward Classes and other needy persons like vocational training in vocations of house hold industries, semi-skilled jobs for self-employment, and to provide food, clothing medical aid, transportation, libraries, laboratories, reading rooms, hostels, play grounds, and other required facilities to the boys/girls and also to the members of the society.
  • 18. To establish & manage various kinds of educational, cultural, social and welfare, vocational training centers in the field of Industrial Scientific medical, technical, agricultural research training institutes to introduce and develop professional courses.
  • 19. To establish and run orphanage centers cr√®ches for the children and working women and hostels for women.
  • 20. To provide and held. assistance to the physically handicapped people/children in respect of artificial limbs and to give them vocational training on different technical/non-technical/professional/industrial and on the other subjects/fields which will make them independent.
  • 21. To prepare the awareness generation kits posters, banners, Audio-video, Cassette, puppet shows, skits, documentaries etc regarding various social issues and for the attainment of the aims and objects of the above society.
  • 22. To publish Books, Charts, illustrations, Journals, magazines, periodicals, News Letters/papers and other publications on the different subjects and different languages for the promotion of above Aims and Objects of the above society.
  • 23. To approach the concerned authority/authorities regarding the solution of the problems of the members of the society.
  • 24. To assist and co-operate the other Association, Societies, Trusts in their Work/Activities whose aims are same like this society.
  • 25. To arrange and manage the training institutions in journalism & Mass Communication, Fashion designing, Computer, Fine Arts, Crafts, Music Painting, Modeling Dancing, Yoga, Physical Education and in other professional training subjects.
  • 26. To approach the farmers and provide them the latest technology and the research information in the fields of agriculture for the betterment of farmers/agriculturists.
  • 27. To make best efforts and organize various kind of programs and / or activities relating to Family planning and to diffuse the useful knowledge about the medicines, equipment, method used for family planning also provide the Family Counseling Facilities.
  • 28. To give, provide and /or render monetary and /or render monetary and/or other help and assistance for the relief of persons and animals affected by natural and other calamities such as flood, fire, famine, cyclone, earth quake, storm, accident, pestilence, drought, epidemic, unbearable cost of living and the like, to give donations, subscriptions or contributions to institutions, establishments, centers or persons doing relief work on such occasion.
  • 29. To make arrangement for mobile emergency VAN.AMBULACE for the victim of road accidents, to provide help to pregnant ladies to shit them to nearby hospital, also to provide assistance to the old people for the emergency.
  • 30. To bring under productive use of the vast land through a massive programs of forestation and tree plantation.
  • 31. To arrange Welfare and research programs for the Indian Public and also provide necessary help/assistance.
  • 32. To open, found, establish promote, set-up, run and maintain and set up of eye-donation camps, eye banks, dispensaries, hospitals and other forms of organization to promote eye donation.
  • 33. To establish, support and grand aid and other financial assistance to medical Institution, Hospitals, Research laboratories and other research centers or and other scientific research institutes for development of the knowledge of human being in subject.
  • 34. To promote, advance and encourage and or aid in helping the common man frm the abuse of drugs and formulate the schemes for ameliorating the drug addicts from the deadly mean of drugs.
  • 35. Grant of medical help to the poor, and grant of medical help to persons effected during famine, floor, earthquake or any calamity or war of warlike operations.
  • 36. To start and maintain infant, children homes, welfare centers and maternity homes for general welfare.
  • 37. To provide best technology for water treatment, and also promote the water resources to awareness in the public to re-use the rain water.
  • 38. To make general public aware about their right on consumer protection & impart their training for such protection.
  • 39. To provide seeds/fertilizers to the farmer/agriculturists for growing the food-grains/flower etc and provide the same on competitive rates to the member.
  • 40. To encourage games/ sports/ yoga/ gymnastics/ judo/ hockey/ football/ cricket and other games for the improvement of health amongst youth and children on the whole by providing play ground, sports clubs, yoga training centers and also organize the tournaments, yoga & sport competitions on block district, level.
  • 41. Society will invest money and funds according to section 11 (5) of the Income Tax Act of 1961.
  • 42. To receive financial and non-financial assistance from Govt/Non-Government organization, international bodies, bank, Company and any other legal entity or individual for attainment the objective of the organization and to fulfill the needs of the people.
  • 43. To accept donations, grant, presents, gifts and other offerings in the shape of movable or immovable properties and the same shall be utilized for the promotion of Aims and objects of the above society.
  • 44. To make survey for collection of dates for the attainment of any objects of the society.
  • 45. To collect notices, notifications , policies, information from the Govt., Semi-Govt., Ngo and other national/International agencies and provide the same to the members of the society and the general public.
  • 46. To erect, construct, alter, maintain improve, develop, manage and control all or any part of the property or the building of the above society, necessary or convenient for the purpose of the attainment of the aims and object of the society.
  • 47. To purchase/acquire the land and/or building in the name of the society for the upliftment and fulfillment of the aims and objects of the society.
  • 48. To do such other things/acts activities which are necessary and which may be incidental or conducive to the attainment of any of the object of the society.
  • 49. All the income, earnings, movable or immovable properties of the society shall be solely utilized and applied towards the promotion of its aims and objects only as set forth in the memorandum of the society and no profit thereof shall be paid or transferred directly or indirectly by way of dividends, bonus, profits or in any manner whatsoever to the present or the past members of the society or to any person claiming through any one or more of the present or the past members. No member of the society shall have any personal claim on any movable or immovable properties of the society or make any profit, whatsoever, by virtue of this membership.

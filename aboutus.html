About us

Chaudhary Pawan Singh Education Welfare Cultural & Charitable Society

Chaudhary Pawan Singh Educational Welfare Cultural & Charitable Society Registered by Government of NCT of Delhi on 24 October 2005 is a Registered Society Bearing Registration No. S/54039/2005 and is purely a Non-Government Organization having a non-political, non-religious and non-commercial character. The Society has been registered under Societies Act 1860.

The main objective of the Chaudhary Pawan Singh Educational Welfare Cultural & Charitable Society is to Promote Education, Skill Development and Employment, Child and Women Empowerment, Public Health, Rural Development, Environmental Protection and Communal Harmony, Livelihood Program and ensure the Socio-Economic Development of the citizens and the country.

From the last 14 years, this Society/Institution has been working on all such issues, and has done commendable work in all fields.

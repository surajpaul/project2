Infrastructure

Chaudhary Pawan Singh Educational, Welfare, Cultural & Charitable Society

We have been able to position ourselves among the leading schools in Delhi/NCR through our offered quality school education and world-class infrastructure facilities. Our institution has been appreciated for over the years by numerous parents owing to our excellent services and discipline level. The school provides state of the art infrastructure and amenities supported by the commitment and experience of the management. The building accommodates spacious and well-ventilated classrooms, seminar halls, activity hall, a library with collection of a large number of books, magazine etc. Medical room with a stationed nurse all the time during the school hours and state of the art fully equipped latest separate labs for students. For our little tiny-tots the infrastructure also comprises of separate nurseries for transforming their innocent childhood into a wonderland of innovation and exploration. Sports playground and activities are an integral part of the campus.

The school offers a wide range of choices and opportunities for both individual and group experiences, as well as for the development of life long skills and interest. Students select from a list of choices when they register for classes each term. Our school seeks to cultivate a sense of National responsibility through its efforts to develop physical education a base for betterment of future generation.

A well equipped modern computer lab with latest technology is available in the school where the children learn technical skills and bring it to use into their application. From the primary wing till the senior wing the children learn the theoretical part and its respective technical application. They learn to operate the technical devices at the very fundamental stages and later apply their learning through projects in the context of real life with the support of expert guidance. They not only learn to work out visual representation, but also learn to demonstrate their learning through verbal presentation individually as well as in a group, which gets followed by a questionnaire session with the involvement of the peer group. This exercise enables them attain the collaborative skill, that is most applied in the era of 21st century. Children are shown videos related to their subject topics or content related movies are shown to them by which they build up their own perspective and viewpoints.

MATHEMATICS LABORATORY

School has established a well equipped Mathematics Laboratory. It is a place where learning becomes fun. The laboratory approach of teaching mathematics provides students with the opportunity to understand and discover the beauty, importance and relevance of mathematics as a discipline. The Maths Lab comprises of a variety of mathematical teaching aids in the form of models, games, charts, CDs, books etc. useful for effective learning.The focus of learning is on developing number sense, spatial sense, recognition of patterns and gradually to attend the problems with algorithm and abstraction. Teachers emphasize on mathematical process which helps the child to imbibe concepts using concrete materials, problem solving with real-life situations, reasoning, reflecting and connecting, selecting appropriate computational tools, strategies and use of mathematical vocabulary.

LIBRARY

A well organized library with a separate reading room for the students and staff is one of the distinctive features of the school. Library operations are computerized which include acquisition of books, cataloguing, circulation and all front desk operations like fine-collection, follow-ups, issue, return, renewal and reservation of books.

The school library has a collection of wonderful and exciting books suited to the needs and tastes of growing children. The Library is equipped with 12,000 books on various subjects ranging from academic subjects like English, Hindi, Maths, Science, Social Studies, Commerce, Computers, Economics to extracurricular non Academic subjects like Sports, Cookery, Fiction, Poetry, Plays, Biographies, Arts etc.

The reading room is regularly updated with useful journals and newspapers for all ages, abilities and aptitudes. We have approx. 1000 reference books. There are 25 periodicals and magazines and 5 dailies in English and Hindi both for students and staff to keep them updated on current affairs. It also houses a collection of teachersâ€™ resource material for better implementation of the curriculum.

SPORTS GROUND

We have a well maintained ground with lush green grass in it. It has appropriate boundaries for safety of the children. We have volleyball court, a Cricket Pitch for net practice and a foot ball Activities.
